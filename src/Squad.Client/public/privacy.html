Domestique Team

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Domestique Team (“we,” “us”) is a training app for cycling and triathlon clubs. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you have. We designed the app to collect only what a team‑training experience needs — and we do not track you across other apps or sell your data.

Data we collect

CategoryWhatWhy
AccountYour name, email address, and account identifier (from Sign in with Apple, Google, or email registration)To create and secure your account
Precise locationGPS position, speed, and elevation while you record a ride — including in the background when a live ride is activeTo record your route and show your position to your teammates during a group ride
Health & fitnessWorkout metrics (distance, pace, power, cadence, heart rate) and, if you connect Apple Health, readings such as resting heart rate, heart‑rate variability, respiratory rate, VO₂ max, sleep, and body weightTo build your training log, health trends, and team leaderboards
PhotosProfile and activity photos you choose to addTo personalize your profile and posts
ContentActivity notes, goals, plans, and messages you send in your squadTo provide the feed, planning, and messaging features
We do not use advertising or third‑party analytics SDKs, and we do not use your data to track you across other companies’ apps or websites. Under Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework, our answer is “No tracking.”

How your data is shared

  • With your teammates. Your activities, position during live rides, and leaderboard standing are visible to members of your squad according to your in‑app Privacy settings (profile visibility, activity‑map visibility, “hide start & finish,” live‑location sharing).
  • Service providers. We host data with Microsoft Azure. Sign‑in is handled by Apple and Google; optional integrations exchange data with Apple Health (on your device) and Garmin Connect (if you connect it).
  • We never sell your data and do not share it for advertising.

Your choices & rights

  • Privacy controls. Settings → Privacy lets you control profile and activity‑map visibility, hide the start/finish of routes, and turn live‑location sharing on or off.
  • Delete your account. Settings → Privacy → “Delete account” permanently deletes your account and all associated data (activities, memberships, messages, photos, and any squads you own). This is irreversible.
  • Export your data. You can request a copy of your data from within the app or by emailing us.
  • Permissions. Location, Bluetooth, Camera, Photos, and Health access are optional and requested only when needed; you can change them anytime in iOS Settings.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our systems.

Children

Domestique Team is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
