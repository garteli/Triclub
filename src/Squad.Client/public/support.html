Frequently asked

How do I record a ride?

Open the Live tab, choose your activity type, and tap Start recording. Recording continues in the background with the screen off so your squad can see you on the live map.

How do I connect a heart‑rate strap, power meter, or radar?

On the Live tab, tap Pair a component and select your Bluetooth sensor. Sensors are optional — the app works without them.

Why does the app need Location and Bluetooth?

Location records your route and shows your position to teammates during a live ride. Bluetooth connects to heart‑rate straps, power meters, and Varia radar. Both are requested only when needed and can be changed anytime in iOS Settings.

How do I sync Apple Health or Garmin?

In Settings, connect Apple Health to import readings like resting heart rate and sleep, or connect Garmin to pull in your activities automatically. Activities are de‑duplicated so nothing is logged twice.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data (activities, memberships, messages, photos, and any squads you own). It cannot be undone.
